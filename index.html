Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is PlantDiseaseID?

Our AI model has an accuracy rate of approximately 92% for common plant diseases when provided with clear images of affected leaves or stems. Accuracy may vary for rare diseases or poor quality images.

What types of plants does it support?

Currently, we support over 200 common garden plants, vegetables, fruits, and ornamental plants. We're constantly expanding our database to include more species.

Is my data and images secure?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your images are only used for disease identification and are not stored permanently or shared with third parties.

Can I use this for commercial farming?

Absolutely! Many farmers and agricultural professionals use our tool for early disease detection in crops. We offer commercial plans with additional features.

How can I improve the identification accuracy?

For best results: take clear, well-lit photos of affected leaves from multiple angles, include both close-ups and whole-plant views, and ensure the plant is properly identified if possible.
